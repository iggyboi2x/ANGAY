🌾 ANGAY
Accessible Nutrition & Goods Assistance for You

A demographic-informed food assistance coordination system that connects foodbanks and barangay communities for equitable, transparent, and needs-responsive aid distribution.


📌 Overview
ANGAY is a digital coordination platform built to address the gap between available food assistance and actual community needs. By leveraging barangay-level demographic data, ANGAY enables foodbanks to make informed distribution decisions — ensuring aid reaches the most vulnerable sectors.


Built in alignment with:

🌱 SDG 2 – Zero Hunger
🏙️ SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities


✨ Features

Barangay Demographic Profiling — Aggregate socio-economic data per barangay (population, seniors, PWDs, children, poverty level)
Foodbank Inventory Management — Track available goods by type, quantity, and target beneficiary
Interactive Barangay Map — Visual demographic stats with sorting and filtering by need
Distribution Proposal & Approval Workflow — Structured coordination between foodbanks and barangays
Distribution Logging & History — Transparent records of all distributions
Reporting Dashboard — Summary statistics and allocation history
